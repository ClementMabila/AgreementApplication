Agreement overview

The Canditate agrees to:

  • Abide by the organization's policies and procedures
  • Observe guidelines for appropriate dress, conduct, communication, and maintaining the workspace
  • Complete any required training(studies) before the internship starts
  • Attend the internship site during scheduled work dates/times, notifying the supervisor of absence or late arrival with sufficient notice prior to start time;
  • Fulfill institution requirements to receive academic credit (if applicable)
  • Complete tasks promptly and to a satisfactory standard
  • Inform the supervisor of any problems or concerns.

The Supervisor and Organization Agree to:

  • Comply with the SA Department of Labor policies on paid/unpaid internships;
  • Adhere to all labor laws;
  • Provide a safe work environment;
  • Conduct appropriate training for the candidate prior to the internship;
  • Assign a mentor for the candidate;
  • Establish a set work schedule and lesson plan for the candidate;
  • Provide the candiadate with periodic feedback and constructive criticism;
  • Ensure the candidate learning goals are addressed;
  • Meet Institution requirements for the student to receive academic credit (if applicable); and
  • Compensate the candidate according to the agreed-upon rate or arrangement.
